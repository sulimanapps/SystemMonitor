# SystemMonitor Pro

A lightweight menu bar app for macOS that shows your system stats at a glance.

## What It Does

SystemMonitor Pro sits in your menu bar and gives you quick access to:

- **CPU usage** - See how hard your processor is working
- **Memory** - Track RAM consumption and pressure
- **Disk space** - Monitor storage across all volumes
- **Network** - Watch upload/download speeds in real-time
- **Cache cleaner** - Free up space by removing temporary files
- **App uninstaller** - Completely remove apps and their leftovers

## Requirements

- macOS 14.0 (Sonoma) or later
- Works on both Apple Silicon and Intel Macs

## Quick Start

1. Download the latest release from [GitHub Releases](https://github.com/sulimanapps/SystemMonitor/releases)
2. Unzip and drag to Applications
3. Launch - the app appears in your menu bar
4. Click the icon to see your system stats
